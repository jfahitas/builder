🆘 Mutual Aid Agreements (Crisis Response)

  • ✓ Purpose: Resource sharing during emergencies
  • ✓ Activation: Crisis-driven with specific protocols
  • ✓ Timeline: Short-term deployment during incidents

🤝 Strategic Partnerships (Capability Building)

  • ✓ Purpose: Long-term capability enhancement
  • ✓ Activation: Ongoing collaboration
  • ✓ Timeline: Sustained cooperation over years
  • ✓ Example: Multi-year partnership for grid resilience

💡 Key Insight

Think of strategic partnerships as "mutual aid for capability building" - instead of sharing resources during emergencies, you're sharing capabilities during ongoing development!
